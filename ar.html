About AR Calling

Accounts Receivable (AR) Calling is an essential process in US Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management. It involves communicating with insurance companies to resolve unpaid claims, manage denials, and ensure prompt reimbursement for healthcare providers.

Common Denial Codes

Sample AR Calling Script

AR Caller: Good morning. This is Sridevi calling from ABC Medical Billing regarding claim status. May I have your name, please?

Representative: This is John from Provider Services.

AR Caller: Thank you, John. Could you please verify the status of claim number 123456 for DOS March 10, 2026?

Representative: The claim was denied due to missing authorization.

AR Caller: Thank you. Could you please provide the denial code and reference number?
